Greetings from Fred Hutch,

As Fred Hutch pursues its mission to eliminate cancer and related diseases, it’s important to us to understand the perspectives of those who work to improve patients’ lives and advance science—physicians, researchers and scientists.

Our research team is asking you, our colleagues in medicine and research, to share your perceptions of Fred Hutch so that we can better communicate recruitment, collaboration and partnership opportunities.

To this end, we would greatly appreciate your participation in a short 10-minute survey. Please know that this is for research purposes only and your responses will be kept anonymous and strictly confidential. Please click here to complete the survey.

Thank you in advance for taking the time to share your opinions with us.
